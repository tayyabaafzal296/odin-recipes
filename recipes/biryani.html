Biryani

Description

Biryani is a rich and aromatic rice dish layered with marinated meat, spices, and saffron. It's a celebration on a plate.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken
  • Yogurt, garlic, ginger paste
  • Biryani masala
  • Fried onions
  • Saffron or food color
  • Fresh coriander and mint

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with spices and yogurt.
  2. Cook rice halfway and layer with chicken.
  3. Top with fried onions, mint, and saffron.
  4. Steam ("dum") on low heat for 20 minutes.
