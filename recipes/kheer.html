Kheer

Description

Kheer is a creamy and sweet rice pudding made with milk, rice, and sugar â€” a classic desi dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Cardamom pods
  • Chopped almonds and pistachios

Steps

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Boil milk and add rice.
  3. Cook slowly on low heat, stirring regularly.
  4. Once thick, add sugar and cardamom.
  5. Garnish with nuts and serve chilled.
