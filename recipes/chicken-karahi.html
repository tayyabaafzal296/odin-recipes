chicken karahi

ingredients
  • 500g chicken
  • 4 tomatoes (blended )
  • 1 onion
  • 2tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 2 green chilllis
  • 1 tsp garam masalla
  • salt to taste
  • fresh coriander
  • cooking oil

steps

  1. heat oil in a pan and fry onions untill golden
  2. add ginger garlic paste and saute for a minute
  3. add chicken and cook untill it turns white
  4. add tomatoes,green chillies,salt and spices
  5. cook on medium heat untill oil separates
  6. garnish white fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice
